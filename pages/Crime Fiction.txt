<i><b>Crime Fiction</b></i> is the genre of fiction that deals with 
crimes, their detection, criminals, and their motives. It is usually 
distinguished from mainstream fiction and other genres such as science 
fiction or historical fiction, but boundaries can be, and indeed are, 
blurred. It has several sub-genres, including detective fiction 
(including the whodunnit), legal thriller, courtroom drama, and 
hard-boiled fiction. <p style="text-align: right; font-size: 84%">—Excerpted from <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Crime_fiction" class="extiw" title="wikipedia:Crime fiction">Crime fiction</a> on <i>Wikipedia</i>, the Free Encyclopedia.</p>
